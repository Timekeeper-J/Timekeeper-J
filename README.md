## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an undergraduate student at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)**, majoring in **Information Security** in the **IEEE Honor Class** at the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering.

Currently, I am exploring **Embodied AI**, with a particular focus on how intelligent agents can perceive, reason, and interact within complex 3D environments. I am especially interested in **scene generation**, **interactive simulation environments**, and the use of generative models to support robotic learning.

I enjoy building systems that connect perception, generation, and action — from visual understanding to physically grounded interaction.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

- **Embodied AI**  
  Developing agents that can understand, reason about, and interact with the physical world.

- **Scene Generation**  
  Leveraging generative models to synthesize large-scale, high-fidelity, and interactive 3D environments for robotic simulation and training.

- **3D Vision & Representation Learning**  
  Exploring 3D perception, spatial understanding, object-centric representations, and scene-level reasoning.

- **Robotic Simulation & Learning Environments**  
  Building realistic virtual environments that can bridge the gap between simulation and real-world robotic deployment.
